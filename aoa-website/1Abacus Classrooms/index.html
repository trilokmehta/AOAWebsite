Rules & Procedures

Please read the following guidelines carefully to ensure a smooth learning experience.

General & Administrative

  • Re-registration: Needed if absent or withdrawn for > 90 days.
  • Batch Management: Center reserves right to merge or split batches.
  • Grade Sheets: Provided to parents. Answer sheets are not shared.
  • Non-Compete: Materials cannot be used for business or by competitor programs for 3 years after enrollment.

Student Responsibilities

  • Classroom Aids: Must bring pencil, eraser, abacus, and workbooks.
  • Reporting Time: Report 5 minutes before class starts.
  • Practice: 15-30 mins, 4 days a week. Complete homework.
  • Consistency: Regular attendance is crucial for development.

Parental Guidelines

  • Monitoring: Sign the back of each workbook weekly.
  • Drop & Pick-up: Must be done inside the center with sign-in/out.
  • Assessment Week: Classes last 1 to 1.5 hours. Pick up early.
  • Contact Info: Notify us immediately of any email/phone changes.

Attendance & Leaves

  • Planned Absence: Notify 2 days in advance (short leave) or 30 days (long).
  • Make-up Classes: Scheduled only if notified 24 hours in advance.
  • Catch-up Classes: For students joining late or falling behind.
  • Holidays: Closings posted 7-14 days prior. Make-Up Days will be arranged.
