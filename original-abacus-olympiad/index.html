THE ORIGINAL ABACUS OLYMPIAD

Where Competition Meets Celebration. Where Every Child Evolves.

Our Genesis & Philosophy

The journey began in 2004. Originally known as the Abacus Championship, it was a testing ground for young minds. Under the visionary leadership of Jay Vardhan Rishi, the event underwent a democratic transformation into The Abacus Olympiad.

“How can we subject a 4-year-old to the ruthless stress of winning and losing? True education should build confidence, not anxiety.”

We turned the arena of stress into a sanctuary of joy, where the goal is improvement, higher mastery, and the thrill of surpassing one’s own previous best.

The Science of Evaluation

Our proprietary 5-Point Evaluation Grid assesses:

  • Tool Maneuvering
  • Visualization
  • Accuracy
  • Speed
  • Mental Stamina

The Promise

"Start Smiling, End Smiling." There are no losers here.

For the Student: By the time the Olympiad ends, you are faster, sharper, and more confident than when you started. That is the true victory.
